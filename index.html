About

Hello and welcome to my website, my name is Rowan. The “Row” rhymes with cow. I attend CSU Long Beach as a graduate student; currently I pursue a Master of Science in Computer Science. I received my Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from UC Santa Barbara. My academic interests include graph theory, 3D graphics, and linear algebra, as well as applications in programming language and OS and program design, among other topics. I have a passion for game engine design as well as competitive balancing. At the moment I TA two sections of CECS 228: Discrete Structures with Computing Applications at CSULB and look to contribute to free and open source software (FOSS) wherever I can. Whenever time allows, I enjoy cooking and exploring the world of food and listening to audiobooks.
